Who we are?

En Steve Tech somo un grupo de desarrollo con mas de 5 anos de experiencia en el análisis de negocios y automatización de procesos. Nuestro objetivo es brindar un beneficio practico y sencillo para poder aplicar de forma efectiva. Si hay una forma mas sencilla de hacerlo, nosotros se lo mostraremos.

What can we do for you?

My company carries out business analysis projects to automate frequent processes or procedures. This translates into investing less time to train staff, greater effectiveness in carrying out processes and fewer errors in daily tasks. Have you had to create the same spreadsheet with the same fields and data day after day, week after week? We create these forms for you so that each day you can update the data or register the company's content without wasting a minute on it. We also carry out analysis of large volumes of data, processing them into easy-to-understand graphs for correct decision-making and effective business analysis.
